🇮🇳 Our Story

Empowering India's Workforce

We believe every skilled worker deserves dignity, opportunity, and fair pay. That's why we built SkillConnect.

Our Mission

Bridging the Skill Gap in India

India has over 300 million skilled workers — the largest skilled workforce in the world. Yet most of them lack access to technology, fair wages, and stable employment opportunities.

SkillConnect was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to use technology to create dignity and opportunity for every skilled worker in India, while helping businesses find quality labour efficiently.
