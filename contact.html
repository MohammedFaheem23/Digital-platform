FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SkillConnect free to use for workers? +

Yes! Registering and creating a profile is completely free for workers. We charge a small service fee only when you successfully get hired through our platform.

How does the verification process work? +

Workers submit their ID proof (Aadhaar) and skill certificates. Our team verifies within 24–48 hours. Verified profiles get 3x more job matches and earn higher trust from employers.

How are payments processed? +

Employers deposit payment into our secure escrow system. Once the job is marked complete by both parties, the amount is released to the worker's bank account within 24 hours.

What if I have a dispute with a worker/employer? +

We have a dedicated dispute resolution team. You can raise a dispute within 72 hours of job completion. Our team will review and resolve issues typically within 3–5 business days.
